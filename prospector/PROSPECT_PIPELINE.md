## Status Definitions

- **FOUND** — Lead identified, matches ICP
- **DRAFT_READY** — Personalized proposal drafted by Prospector, awaiting Moises review
- **APPROVED** — Moises approved proposal; ready to send
- **SENT** — Proposal sent to client
- **REPLIED** — Client responded (positive or negative)
- **NEGOTIATING** — In conversation, discussing scope/price
- **CLOSED_WON** — Contract signed, project started
- **CLOSED_LOST** — Rejected or non-responsive
- **ON_HOLD** — Paused (client resources, timing, etc.)

---

## Priority Rules

**Highest Priority (send first):**
- Score 8–10
- Budget $2K+
- Reply within 24 hours
- Status: DRAFT_READY or APPROVED

**Medium Priority:**
- Score 6–7
- Budget $1K–$2K
- Status: SENT, awaiting reply

**Low Priority (backlog):**
- Score <6
- <$1K budget
- Status: ON_HOLD or CLOSED_LOST (archive after 30 days)

## Notes Template

For each lead, capture:
```
- **ICP Fit:** (Tier 1–3 / Quick Win)
- **Urgency:** (ASAP / This month / Flexible)
- **Automation Opportunity:** (Brief description)
- **Contact:** (Name, email, phone if available)
- **Competitor Count:** (Upwork: X proposals)
- **Moises Review:** (Approved / Revise / Reject with reason)
- **Proposal Sent:** (Date)
- **Follow-up Needed:** (Yes/No, by date)
```
